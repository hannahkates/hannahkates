Using data and civic tech to help cities & governments operate more efficiently, sustainably, and equitably

I'm currently a product manager and web developer on the NYC Planning Labs team at NYC Department of City Planning. My background is in urban planning and environmental engineering, with experience spanning consulting, non-profit advocacy, and tech startups.

I also paint.

Paintings

My abstract, mixed media paintings draw inspiration from things I've always been drawn to: landscapes, organic forms, minerals, maps, repetitive patterns, and mosaics. I combine those inspirations in colorful and metallic compositions. In the hectic environment of New York City, I enjoy creating order and balance in constellations of shapes.
