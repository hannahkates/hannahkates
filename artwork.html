Paintings

Creating order & balance in constellations of shapes

My abstract, acrylic paintings draw inspiration from things I've always been drawn to: landscapes, organic forms, minerals, maps, and mosaics. I combine those inspirations in colorful and metallic compositions. In the hectic environment of New York City, I enjoy creating order and balance in constellations of shapes.
