Artwork

Creating order & balance in constellations of shapes

My abstract, acrylic paintings and embroidery draw inspiration from things I've always been attracted to: landscapes, organic forms, minerals, maps, and mosaics. I combine those influences in colorful and metallic compositions. In the hectic environment of New York City (and soon-to-be Lagos, Nigeria), I enjoy creating order and balance in constellations of shapes.
